About us

Welcome to Beaglism: A Movement Like No Other! At Beaglism, we create initiatives to raise awareness about the beagle dog breed. Our mission is to spotlight the mistreatment of these beloved companions and educate the public about beagles. We aim to spread happiness, joy, and contentment for our cherished friends. This movement welcomes everyoneâ€”whether you have a beagle or not, whether you're a dog owner or simply a dog lover. Join us in spreading the Beaglism movement!



Our mission is driven by these core goals:

Raise Awareness: Educate the public about the common mistreatment of beagles, particularly in research facilities and puppy mills, and promote responsible breeding practices.

Ethical Treatment: Work with lawmakers and organizations to strengthen animal welfare laws that protect beagles and all dogs from cruelty and neglect.

Support Rescue Efforts: Partner with beagle rescue organizations to help rehome abandoned, surrendered, and rescued beagles, giving them a second chance at a loving home.

Promote Responsible Ownership: Provide resources and information to help current and prospective beagle owners understand the breed's specific needs, temperament, and care requirements.

Build Community: Create a welcoming space for beagle lovers to connect, share stories, and support each other in providing the best possible lives for these wonderful dogs.

-For all the beagles worldwide
